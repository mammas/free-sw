Your Data- Under Your Control

With ownCloud you can sync and share your files, calendar, contacts and more. Access your data from all your devices, on an open platform you can extend and modify. ownCloud protects your data by giving you complete control over it; you decide what is shared with who and for how long. You also have the ability to access your data from your web browser, Android or iOS devices and your laptop and desktop. Furthermore, you can add apps, external storage, or even write your own code! Join the friendly open source community and get started!

Protect

ownCloud used encryption to protect your data; encryption is the encoding of data in a way that makes it nearly impossible for somebody else to read it without a specific piece of knowledge. It encrypts the transfering of your data to and from your server, and the external storage itself.ownCloud uses encryption to protect your data when it is not on the ownCloud client or the server. However, it does not encrypt data on your computer or protect you if access to your ownCloud server itself is compromised. The ownCloud Encryption app provides a great way to secure your data on untrusted cloud storage services. It is easy to enable and works entirely unnoticeable for the user.

Store your files, folders, contacts, photo galleries, calendars and more on a server of your choosing. Access that folder from your mobile device, your desktop, or a web browser. You can get the most recent version of your files with the desktop and web client or mobile app of your choosing, at any time. Share your data with others, and give them access to your latest photo galleries or anything else you want them to see. Share it publicly, or privately. It is your data, do what you want with it.
