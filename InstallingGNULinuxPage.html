What is a Distribution?

A distribution is essentially a set of packages which is attached to a kernel and surrounding core utilities in order to carter to a nitch of society. For example, you have Musix for music artist.

Are These Distributions Entirely Free?

Yes. Developers have made a committment to free and open source software, therefore these ready-to-use distributions are entirely free and abide by the four freedoms of libre software. The aboved mention distributions are completely free by the Free Software Foundation's standards, however they are not the only GNU/Linux distributions out there.
